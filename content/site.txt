Description:

Alejandro Matamala Ortiz.
I'm a freelance interactive designer who also code some projects. Actually, I'm pursuing a Master degree in Interactive Telecommunications - ITP - at the New York University. I've worked with interesting clients and institutions, such as Levi's, Government of Chile, Banco Estado, Universidad Católica de Chile, Universidad Adolfo Ibañez and collaborated with great artists, designers and galleries. I Co-Founded a couple of startups in e-commerce and healthcare, a Design Studio in Santiago, and I taught a project design class at Universidad del Pacífico. Currently, Sebastián Rodríguez, Jorge Losse and I run an independent art book publishing project Ediciones Daga.
